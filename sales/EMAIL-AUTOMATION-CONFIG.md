### **Automation Rules**

**Engagement Scoring**:
- Email opened: +5 points
- Link clicked: +10 points
- Demo link clicked: +15 points
- Calendar booking: +25 points
- Email replied: +20 points

**Workflow Triggers**:
- High engagement (>30 points): Assign to sales rep for immediate follow-up
- Demo link clicked: Send demo preparation email
- Calendar booked: Create high-priority deal in CRM
- Email bounced: Update contact information and retry

**Sequence Exits**:
- Meeting booked: Exit sequence, enter demo follow-up
- Unsubscribe: Remove from all sequences
- Hard bounce: Mark as invalid, remove from database
- Competitor mentioned: Flag for competitive analysis
